BMI calculator

BMI means body mass index. It shows that if a person is healthy or not. He/She can change their lifestyle according their BMI index.

BMI Calculator Chart

BMI Weight Status
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5-24.9 Healthy
25.0-29.9 Overweight
30.0- and above Obese

Calculate Your BMI

You can calculate your BMI using your weight(KG) and height(Meter). Divide your weight by the square of your height and you will find your BMI.
